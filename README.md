# Project Overview: Smart Inventory & Price Optimization App

## Executive Summary
Our project is a cutting-edge mobile application designed to revolutionize inventory management and financial tracking for small and medium-sized businesses. By eliminating manual data entry, the app streamlines operational workflows, analyzes costs in real-time, and leverages automated pricing strategies to maximize profitability.

---

## Key Features

* **AI-Powered Invoice Scanning:** Users can simply take a photo of their purchase invoices or receipts. The app automatically extracts all relevant data using advanced optical character recognition (OCR), eliminating the need for manual typing.
* **Instant Cost Reporting:** The moment an invoice is scanned, the system updates the inventory and generates comprehensive expense and cost reports in real-time.
* **Automated Price Curve Optimization:** The app analyzes historical cost data and market trends to automatically calculate and suggest the optimal pricing curve for products, ensuring maximum margins.
* **Streamlined Operations:** A fast, intuitive mobile experience focused on saving time and reducing human error in day-to-day business management.
